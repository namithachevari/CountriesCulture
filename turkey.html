Welcome to Turkey!

Turkey is a transcontinental country located at the crossroads of Europe and Asia. It is bordered by eight countries, including Greece, Bulgaria, and Armenia to the west, and Iran, Iraq, and Syria to the east. The country has a rich cultural heritage and a long history, with evidence of human settlement dating back thousands of years. Today, Turkey is a diverse and modern nation with a population of over 80 million people. It is a popular tourist destination, with attractions such as Istanbul's historic Old City, the stunning Mediterranean coastline, and the unique landscapes of Cappadocia. The country is also known for its delicious cuisine, which blends Middle Eastern, Mediterranean, and Central Asian influences. Whether you're interested in history, culture, or natural beauty, Turkey has something to offer everyone.
